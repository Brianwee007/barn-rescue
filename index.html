01 — The idea

Most barns aren't broken.
They're just running blind.

Beautiful barns. Great horses. Owners working eighty-hour weeks — and somehow still losing money. We've seen it over and over. The riding is almost never the problem.

The problem is everything around it: the pricing, the schedule, the way new families are welcomed (or aren't), the cash that quietly leaks out every month. Fix those, and a barn that was barely surviving turns into something families fight to stay in.

We find barns worth backing — and then we actually back them.

02 — What partnering looks like

i.

A real plan, not a pep talk

We rebuild how your barn actually makes money — pricing, packages, the day-to-day flow — around what your families truly want.

ii.

Hands-on operators

Not consultants who email you a deck. People who show up, work alongside your team, and get hard things done.

iii.

Capital where it counts

For the right barns, we invest our own resources to remove the things holding you back — so growth isn't gated by your bank balance.

We can only work with a few barns at a time, so not every applicant will be selected. The application below tells us whether your barn is a fit.

03 — Who should apply

If any of this sounds like you, apply.

  • You own or run a barn or riding school in Ohio.
  • You're full of lessons but the money never seems to follow.
  • You're doing the work of five people and want to build a real business, not just a job.
  • You'd say yes to outside help — and outside investment — if it came from people who actually understood barns.
